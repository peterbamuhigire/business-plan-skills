# Cement Brick Making

**UNDP Ref:** #176 | **Sub-sector:** Metal Products & Hardware
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 52,000 blocks/month
- **Key Equipment:** Block-making machine, cement mixer, vibrator, moulds, wheelbarrows

## Market

Construction sector with strong acceptance for both hollow and solid blocks. Demand driven by Uganda's ongoing urbanisation and building activity.
