## Market

The oil and gas sector is a growing industry in Uganda with high demand for quality catering services. A typical oil well team consists of 200–400 people located on isolated oil fields away from normal towns. The Albertine Graben development (Tilenga and Kingfisher projects, East African Crude Oil Pipeline) drives demand through the late 2020s. Oil companies require certified food safety and hygiene standards (HACCP or equivalent), and contracts are typically awarded through competitive tender. Menu planning must account for diverse dietary requirements given international crews.
