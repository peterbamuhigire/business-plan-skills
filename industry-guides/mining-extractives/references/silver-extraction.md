## Market

Silver is a precious metal extensively used in photography, X-ray films, jewellery, electrical materials, and medicine. Extraction from waste materials represents a circular economy opportunity. This is a niche operation requiring technical knowledge of silver recovery chemistry. Revenue depends heavily on commodity prices (approximately US$ 25–35 per troy ounce as of 2026) and quality of waste feedstock.
