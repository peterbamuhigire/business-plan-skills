# Cement Brick Making

**UNDP Ref:** #176 | **Sub-sector:** Bricks & Building Materials (Mining-Adjacent)
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Production of cement bricks (hollow and solid blocks) for the construction industry, bridging mining (aggregate/sand extraction) and building materials supply.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |

| Capital Investment | $15,300 |

| Payback Period | N/A |

## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 52,000 blocks per month
- **Key Equipment:** Cement block-making machine, cement mixing machine, coffee tray, vibrator, moulds, wheelbarrows

## Market

Bricks are a basic requirement for any construction activity. Cement bricks (both hollow and solid) have strong market acceptance because of their structural strength. Listed under the Mining Sector in the source document because of the raw material extraction component (sand, aggregate). The 39% profit margin assumes efficient production and stable input costs.
