## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 2,600 kits per month; 31,200 kits per year
- **Key Equipment:** Assembly and packaging of: plastic tweezers, disposable gloves, gauze, adhesive bandages, dressings, saline, soap, antiseptic wipes — into durable plastic boxes, fabric pouches, or wall-mounted cabinets

## Market

First aid kits are essential collections of supplies and equipment for administering first aid, with contents varying by region due to differing legislation and organisational requirements. Key customers include workplaces (required by occupational health regulations), schools, vehicles (required by traffic regulations), households, and NGOs. Distribution can target both institutional buyers (bulk orders) and retail consumers.
