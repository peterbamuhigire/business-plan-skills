## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 1 tonne of syringes per day; 30 tonnes per month
- **Key Equipment:** Disposable syringe moulding machine, syringe packaging machine, fixed data equipment

## Market

High demand for disposable syringes across Uganda's health sector — hospitals, clinics, health centres, and vaccination programmes. Local production reduces dependence on imports and benefits from the "Buy Uganda Build Uganda" policy. Quality certification from UNBS and NDA is essential for market access, and production must comply with FDA specifications and good manufacturing practices (GMP).
