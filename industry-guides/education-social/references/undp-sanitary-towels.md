## Market

Sanitary towels are essential hygiene products for women and girls, with menstrual hygiene management remaining a significant barrier to girls' school attendance in Uganda and across East Africa — studies estimate girls miss 2-3 school days per month due to lack of affordable products. Local production reduces cost compared to imports, and the business combines commercial viability with strong social impact, making it attractive to impact investors, NGO partnerships, and government programmes promoting girls' education.
