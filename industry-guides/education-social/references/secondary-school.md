# Secondary School

**UNDP Ref:** #200 | **Sub-sector:** Private Education
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

## Operations

- **Capacity:** 150 day scholars; up to 1,000 boarding students at full capacity (years 4-5)
- **Key Staff:** Headmaster, deputy head teachers, director of studies, 2 matrons/patrons, 10 subject heads, 10-15 school teachers, bursar, support staff
- **Key Infrastructure:** Classrooms, science laboratories (physics, chemistry, biology), dormitories, sports facilities, teachers' and staff houses, administration block, library, computer lab, school bus, water supply system

## Market

Uganda's increasing population drives large numbers of pupils into secondary schools. The Universal Primary Education (UPE) programme has boosted primary completion rates, feeding demand at secondary level. This model targets pupils from middle-class families willing to pay a premium for quality education, with revenue growing from $877,800 in year 1 to $3,686,200 by years 4-5 as enrolment scales through the phased approach.
