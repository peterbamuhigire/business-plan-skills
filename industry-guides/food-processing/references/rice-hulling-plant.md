# Rice Hulling Plant

**Sub-sector:** Cereals, Grains & Flour
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Mechanical hulling of paddy rice into polished white rice for domestic and export markets.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Capital Investment | US$ 13,800 |
| Annual Revenue | US$ 2,892,600 |
| Annual Operating Costs | US$ 1,439,900 |
| Annual Profit | US$ 1,452,600 |
| Profit Margin | 50% |

## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 7,200 kg/day
- **Key Equipment:** Combined Rice huller, Electric Motor, Truck, Weighing scale

## Market

Local rice competes with imports on freshness and price. South Sudan provides an additional export market. Growing domestic rice consumption supports expansion.
