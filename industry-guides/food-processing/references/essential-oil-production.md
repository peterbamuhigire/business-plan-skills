# Essential Oil Production

**Sub-sector:** Oils, Fats & Extracts
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Fractional distillation of plant materials to produce essential oils for pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and food industries.

## Operations

- **Key Equipment:** Fractional Distillation with Condenser, Steam generating tank, Truck (3-tonne), Lab equipment

## Market

Pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and food flavouring industries are the primary buyers. Export potential exists for high-quality essential oils from Ugandan botanicals.
