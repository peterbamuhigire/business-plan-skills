# Coconut Cream

**Sub-sector:** Dairy & Dairy Products
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Extraction and processing of coconut cream for bakery, confectionery, and chocolate industries.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Capital Investment | US$ 34,600 |
| Annual Revenue | US$ 5,999,500 |
| Annual Operating Costs | US$ 4,787,400 |
| Annual Profit | US$ 1,212,100 |
| Profit Margin | 20% |

## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 74,984 kg/month
- **Key Equipment:** Hammer mill, Screw Press, Storage tanks, Vibrating sieve, Emulsifier

## Market

Bakery, confectionery, and chocolate industries are the primary buyers. Export potential exists for processed coconut cream to regional markets.
