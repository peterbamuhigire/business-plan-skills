# Powder Milk Production

**Sub-sector:** Dairy & Dairy Products
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Large-scale spray-drying of fresh milk into powder milk for extended shelf life and broad distribution.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Capital Investment | US$ 104,600 |
| Annual Revenue | US$ 3,962,400 |

| Annual Profit | US$ 47,300 |
| Profit Margin | 1% |

## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 624,000 kg/year
- **Key Equipment:** Spray drying, pasteuriser, packaging machinery

## Market

Ready market across Uganda due to long shelf life advantage. Powder milk serves both household consumers and food manufacturers requiring stable dairy inputs.
