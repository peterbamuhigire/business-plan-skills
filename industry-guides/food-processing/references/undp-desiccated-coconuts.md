# Desiccated Coconuts

**Sub-sector:** Oils, Fats & Extracts
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Processing of fresh coconuts into desiccated coconut for bakery and confectionery use.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Capital Investment | US$ 13,580 |
| Annual Revenue | US$ 231,800 |

| Annual Profit | US$ 46,800 |

## Operations

- **Key Equipment:** Disintegrator, De-husking tool, Sieving machine, Grinder

## Market

High demand in bakeries and confectioneries for desiccated coconut as a baking ingredient. Extended shelf life compared to fresh coconut enables wider distribution.
