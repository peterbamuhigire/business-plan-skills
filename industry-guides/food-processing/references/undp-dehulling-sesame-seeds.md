# Dehulling Sesame Seeds

**Sub-sector:** Cereals, Grains & Flour
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Processing of raw sesame seeds into dehulled form for domestic use and export markets.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Capital Investment | US$ 4,950 |
| Annual Revenue | US$ 257,600 |
| Annual Operating Costs | US$ 181,350 |
| Annual Profit | US$ 76,200 |
| Profit Margin | 30% |

## Operations

- **Key Equipment:** Soaking Tank, Pulper, Drier, Trays, Weighing scale

## Market

Uganda is a significant sesame producer. Dehulled seeds command export premiums in international markets, while domestic demand comes from food processors and bakeries.
