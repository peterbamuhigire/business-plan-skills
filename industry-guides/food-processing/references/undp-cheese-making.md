# Cheese Making

**Sub-sector:** Dairy & Dairy Products
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Production of cheese from fresh milk for supply to hotels, restaurants, and urban retail outlets.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Capital Investment | US$ 26,000 |
| Annual Revenue | US$ 1,188,700 |
| Annual Operating Costs | US$ 604,300 |
| Annual Profit | US$ 584,500 |
| Profit Margin | 49% |

## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 1,000 kg/day
- **Key Equipment:** Milk Van, Cream Separator, cheese moulds

## Market

Hotels, restaurants, and urban retail are the primary customers. Cheese production serves as import substitution, replacing expensive imported varieties with locally produced alternatives.
