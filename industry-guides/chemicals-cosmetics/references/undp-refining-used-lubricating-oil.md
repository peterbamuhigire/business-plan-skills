# Refining Used Lubricating Oil

**UNDP Ref:** #165 | **Sub-sector:** Petroleum & Lubricant Products
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Collecting and refining used lubricating oil for resale.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Capital Investment | $21,300 |
| Annual Revenue | $160,500 |
| Annual Operating Costs | $142,500 |
| Annual Profit | $18,000 |
| Profit Margin | 20% |

## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 3,500 litres per month
- **Key Equipment:** Storage tank, settling tank, acid treatment tank, vacuum distillation, receiver for fuel dilution, gas absorber, horizontal plate condenser

## Market

High-value recycled product with growing demand. Environmental benefit from proper disposal of used oil. Requires environmental compliance (NEMA) for handling used oil and chemical waste.
