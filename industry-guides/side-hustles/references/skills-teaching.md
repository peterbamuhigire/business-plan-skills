# Skills Teaching and Coaching Side Hustles

**Audience:** EA professionals with expertise others want to learn
**Sources:** Guillebeau (2017), Amitabh (2022), Gary Raj (2022)

> Teaching what you know is the most ethically defensible and personally satisfying side hustle. If you have spent 3–10 years mastering a skill in a corporate career, you are more qualified to teach it than 90% of the people in your network. The challenge is not expertise — it is packaging, marketing, and pricing.

## 1. Academic Tutoring

**Highest-demand subjects (Uganda and Kenya):**

| Subject | Level | Rate (UGX/hr) |
|---------|-------|--------------|
| Mathematics | O-Level, A-Level | 30,000–80,000 |
| Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) | O-Level, A-Level | 30,000–80,000 |
| English (composition, comprehension) | Primary, O-Level | 20,000–50,000 |
| Economics / Commerce | O-Level, A-Level | 25,000–60,000 |
| Computer Studies / ICT | O-Level | 25,000–70,000 |
| University-level accounting | University | 50,000–150,000/hr |
| ACCA / CPA exam preparation | Professional | 80,000–200,000/hr |

**Starting:**
1. Announce availability in your neighbourhood WhatsApp groups and school alumni networks
2. Offer first session free to a test student; use their results as your testimonial
3. Group sessions (3–6 students, same level) at UGX 20,000/student = UGX 60,000–120,000/hour — more time-efficient than 1-on-1

**Where to find students:** School WhatsApp groups; church community boards; employer family connections; university halls of residence; parental networks on Facebook.

**High-value niche:** ACCA/CPA/CMA professional accounting exam preparation. A tutor who has passed these exams and can teach working professionals in the evenings commands premium rates (UGX 100,000–200,000/hour) because the student body is employed adults investing in career advancement.

## 2. Language Teaching

**Demand in EA cities:**
- **English for professionals** — Executives and civil servants improving business English; speech, writing, presentation skills
- **French** — Growing demand in Rwanda, Uganda, DRC corridor; bilingual preference for NGO/UN jobs
- **Swahili** — Ugandans and Rwandans learning Swahili for East African Community career mobility
- **Chinese (Mandarin)** — Growing demand in Uganda due to Chinese investment; rare skill = premium rates
- **Arabic** — Religious community demand; growing for business with Middle East traders

**Rates (Kampala, 2026):**
- Group language class (6–10 students): UGX 50,000–150,000/class/student/month
- 1-on-1 professional English coaching: UGX 80,000–200,000/session
- Online via Zoom (international students): $15–$30/hr

**Online language teaching — international market:**
- Platforms: Italki, Preply, TeachMeAfrican (for African language learners internationally)
- Teaching African languages (Luganda, Swahili, Kinyarwanda) to diaspora and international learners — unique skill set with minimal competition
- Payment: Payoneer or platform transfer; $15–$40/hr for African languages to diaspora learners

## 3. Professional Skills Workshops

**The model:** Design a 1-day or half-day workshop teaching a specific professional skill; charge per seat; run on Saturdays.

**High-demand workshop topics for EA professionals:**

| Topic | Target Audience | Seats × Price | Revenue |
|-------|----------------|--------------|---------|
| Excel for Business (intermediate) | Accountants, admin staff | 15 × UGX 80,000 | UGX 1,200,000 |
| Personal Finance and Investment | Young professionals | 20 × UGX 50,000 | UGX 1,000,000 |
| Proposal and Report Writing | NGO/civil service staff | 15 × UGX 100,000 | UGX 1,500,000 |
| Social Media Marketing for SMEs | Small business owners | 20 × UGX 80,000 | UGX 1,600,000 |
| Job Search and Interview Skills | Graduates, job seekers | 30 × UGX 40,000 | UGX 1,200,000 |
| Starting a Side Hustle | Corporate employees | 20 × UGX 100,000 | UGX 2,000,000 |
| Public Speaking and Presentation | Professionals, managers | 15 × UGX 120,000 | UGX 1,800,000 |
| Basic Bookkeeping for SMEs | Small business owners | 20 × UGX 80,000 | UGX 1,600,000 |

**Logistics:**
- Venue: church halls, hotel conference rooms (some rent for UGX 100,000–300,000/day); co-working spaces
- Marketing: Facebook Events, WhatsApp broadcasts, LinkedIn posts, physical flyers at supermarkets and offices
- Materials: printed handouts (optional) or WhatsApp shared PDF

**Revenue after costs (UGX 1,200,000 gross, 20 participants):**
- Venue: UGX 200,000
- Printing/materials: UGX 50,000
- Refreshments: UGX 150,000
- **Net: UGX 800,000 for 1 Saturday** — equivalent to half a month's junior professional salary

**Scaling:** Run the same workshop monthly; improve content each time; develop a series (Level 1 → Level 2); sell recordings online.

## 4. Online Courses (Pre-Recorded)

**The 1,000 True Fans economics (Amitabh, 2022):**
You do not need millions of students. 200 students paying UGX 50,000/course = UGX 10,000,000. Create once; sell indefinitely.

**Platform options for EA creators:**

| Platform | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| Udemy | Large global audience; no marketing effort | Low prices ($10–$30); high competition; Udemy takes 50–63% commission |
| Teachable | You set prices; professional look; email list ownership | Monthly fee ($39–$119/month); you do marketing |
| Gumroad | Simple; free to start (takes 10%); good for PDFs and simple videos | Basic features |
| WhatsApp/Telegram course | No platform fees; maximum control; familiar to EA audience | Manual delivery; no automation |
| YouTube (free course) | Large audience; monetise with ads and coaching upsell | Can't charge for videos directly; income from ads is low |

**Recommended EA approach:**
1. Create your course content (video + PDF notes) using phone and laptop
2. Host on Gumroad or Teachable (or Google Drive + Stripe/Pesapal for payment)
3. Sell via WhatsApp, Facebook, LinkedIn, and your email/contact list
4. Price in UGX for local market; USD for international (Payoneer for payment)

**Production:**
- Record using phone (good lighting, quiet room, clean background)
- Screen record your computer for Excel/software tutorials (Loom is free)
- Edit in CapCut (free) or iMovie
- No camera needed for audio-only courses (podcast-style) — record on phone

## 5. Professional Coaching and Mentoring

**What it is:** One-on-one guidance for career progression, business strategy, leadership development, or personal performance.

**Who coaches:** Professionals with 7+ years of experience in a domain who have achieved measurable results (promotions, business growth, problem-solving record).

**Who hires coaches:**
- Mid-career professionals stuck in their growth seeking career acceleration
- Entrepreneurs needing business strategy guidance
- New managers transitioning from individual contributor to team leader
- Job seekers needing interview preparation and confidence
- Young professionals seeking mentors they cannot access freely

**Pricing (Kampala/Nairobi, 2026):**
- Peer professional: UGX 100,000–200,000/session (60 minutes)
- Experienced professional (10+ years, senior role): UGX 200,000–500,000/session
- Executive/C-suite: UGX 500,000–1,500,000/session

**Package structures are more reliable than per-session billing:**
- 6-session career acceleration package: UGX 600,000–1,200,000
- 12-week business strategy programme: UGX 1,500,000–3,000,000

**How to start:**
1. Identify 3 people in your network who would benefit from your specific expertise
2. Offer 1 free "discovery session" (30 minutes) — coach them on one specific problem
3. If value is clear, propose a 4-session package
4. Build testimonials; increase rate after 5 paying clients
5. Create a simple LinkedIn post describing the transformation you help clients achieve

**The Passion Economy positioning (Amitabh, 2022):** Build a "Category of One" — not just "a business coach" but "a finance professional who helps chartered accountants transition into executive roles in Kampala." Specificity commands premium rates and eliminates price comparison.

## Risk and Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Low student turnout for workshops | Pre-sell minimum 10 seats before booking venue; refund if under minimum |
| Students demanding refunds on online courses | Clear refund policy; deliver exactly what was promised; respond to complaints fast |
| Reputational risk if teaching outside expertise | Only teach what you have actually done; cite your practical experience clearly |
| Platform closing or changing commission structure | Build your own email/WhatsApp list independent of any platform |
| Employer conflict (teaching competitors' staff) | Avoid teaching in your direct industry sector; check employment contract |
