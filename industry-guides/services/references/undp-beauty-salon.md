## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 30 customers per day
- **Key Equipment:** Water kettle, towels, sink, aprons, furniture, shavers, fan, wall styling mirrors

## Market

Beauty salons serve a consistent and growing customer base, particularly in urban areas. Low capital requirement and quick payback make this accessible for individual entrepreneurs. Success depends on skills, location, and building a loyal client base through quality service.
