## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 4,000 shock absorbers per annum
- **Key Equipment:** Oil fired tilting furnace, weighing balance, ladle and tongs, hand moulding equipment, bench grinder, mixing machine

## Market

Uganda's road conditions create consistent demand for shock absorber repair and reconditioning. The service is significantly cheaper than replacement, making it attractive for commercial vehicle operators and boda-boda (motorcycle taxi) riders who need to keep vehicles operational at lowest cost.
