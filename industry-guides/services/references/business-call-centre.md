## Market

Call centres enable customers to place orders online, conduct transactions, make payments, order loans, and download digital products. Services include web integration, automatic call distribution, interactive voice response, predictive dialler, and management features. Uganda's English-speaking workforce and competitive labour costs make it attractive for outsourced services.
