## Fuel & Distribution Services

### Mobile Fuel Distribution (Idea 153)

| Item | Value |
|---|---|

| **Annual Revenue** | $554,700 |
| **Annual Operating Costs** | $487,900 |
| **Annual Profit** | $66,900 |
| **Profit Margin** | 12% |
| **Payback Period** | 5 months |
| **Key Equipment** | Delivery van (2.5 tonnes), fuel tank (1,500 litres), funnel, furniture and fixtures |

**Market Context:** Fuel is a commodity used by almost every household.
