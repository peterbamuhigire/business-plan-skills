## Market

Fuel is a commodity used by almost every household. Taking fuel distribution services to underserved rural and semi-urban areas — especially kerosene — is a profitable venture as rural consumers often pay significantly higher prices due to distance from formal stations. Compliance with fuel handling and safety regulations is essential.
