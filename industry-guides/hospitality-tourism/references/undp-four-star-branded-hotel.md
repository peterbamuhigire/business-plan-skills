## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 70 rooms; 50-70% occupancy in first 2 years, then full capacity at 70 guests/day
- **Key Equipment:** Land, hotel building (70 rooms), 100-seat conference room, health club with sauna and gym, swimming pools (adult and children), 2 hotel shuttles, parking (30-40 vehicles)

## Market

Growth and development of Uganda's tourism sector drives demand. Uganda attracts foreign visitors year-round, while government officials, diplomats, and foreign investors attending meetings and conferences form a steady corporate market. Franchise model provides brand recognition and booking systems.
