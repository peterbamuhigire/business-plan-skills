## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 52 apartments sold per week (time-share model)
- **Key Equipment:** Land, multi-phase buildings (constructed over 6 years), pool and gym, wall fence, website

## Market

Targets middle-class families wanting to reduce vacation costs. Apartments paid weekly or monthly; ownership lasts for life. Annual maintenance fee ~$1,270 plus ~$250 time-share exchange club fee.
