**UNDP Ref:** #82 | **Sub-sector:** Food Service & Catering
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Modern roadside restaurant serving food and beverages to local customers and travellers along busy routes.

## Operations

- **Key Equipment:** Fridges, cutlery sets, furniture, cooking equipment, music system, TV and computer, blenders, food warmers, juice mixers and flasks

## Market

High demand for food and beverages in Uganda. A modern restaurant in a well-located area (major highway, trading centre) can be highly profitable. This model serves both local customers and travellers along busy routes.
