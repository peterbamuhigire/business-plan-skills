# Mobile Food Vending

**UNDP Ref:** #83 | **Sub-sector:** Food Service & Catering
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Van-based food delivery service bringing cooked meals directly to offices, construction sites, and industrial areas.

## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 200 covers daily
- **Key Equipment:** Distribution vans, cooking pans, warmers, plates, glasses, cutlery, dish washer, wipers, trays

## Market

Market potential is strong as hotels and restaurants are often expensive and located away from workplaces. Mobile food vendors bring meals directly to offices, construction sites, and industrial areas.
