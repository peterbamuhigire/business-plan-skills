## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 5 movie shows per day + 4 football/sports shows on weekends
- **Key Equipment:** Screen, video player, decoder, air conditioners (4), chairs (200), computer, LCD projector, amplifier, table

## Market

Market spans urban centres and rural areas. Football matches (Premier League, Champions League, AFCON), sports events, music shows, and dramas attract paying viewers. Low capital entry point with strong margins.
