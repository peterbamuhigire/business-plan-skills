## Market

Growing demand from developers interested in well-designed compounds for modern homesteads. Services extend to universities, homes, schools, recreation centres, hospitals, and car parks. Includes ongoing maintenance contracts, which provide recurring revenue. This is a service business with relatively low capital requirements but high dependence on skilled labour and design capability. The 56% margin is achievable for service businesses with low material costs.
