## Market

Multi-storey car parks remain a largely untapped business in Uganda. Located in busy city centre areas with wide catchment. Multiple revenue streams (parking, advertising, towing) improve financial resilience. This is a capital-intensive infrastructure project. Land costs in Kampala's central business district may exceed the estimate. Construction costs should be validated with current contractor quotations.
