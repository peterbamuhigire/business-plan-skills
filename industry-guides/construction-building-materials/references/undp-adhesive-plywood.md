# Making Adhesive Plywood

**UNDP Ref:** #59 | **Sub-sector:** Wood & Timber Products
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Manufacturing adhesive plywood with improved moisture resistance for construction and furniture sectors.

## Financial Summary

| Metric | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Capital Investment | $61,600 |
| Annual Revenue | $2,734,100 |
| Annual Operating Costs | $2,505,900 |
| Annual Profit | $228,200 |
| Profit Margin | 8% |
| Payback Period | 2 months |

## Operations

- **Key Equipment:** Steam jacketed kettle, condenser, receiving tank (30 HP), vacuum pump

## Market

Plywood is widely used in construction for shuttering, partitioning, furniture, and interior finishing. Growing demand from the construction and furniture sectors. Adhesive plywood offers improved moisture resistance. The 2-month payback period is plausible given high throughput (300 pieces per day), but the 8% margin means profitability is highly sensitive to raw material costs and production efficiency.
