# Manufacturing Half Bricks

**UNDP Ref:** #91 | **Sub-sector:** Bricks, Blocks & Cement Products
**Source:** UNDP Uganda Compendium Vol. 2 (adjusted to March 2026 USD, ×1.27)

> Manufacturing half bricks for residential and commercial construction.

| Metric | Amount |

| Capital Investment | $26,000 |

| Payback Period | N/A |

## Operations

- **Key Equipment:** Double shaft mixer, truck (3 tonnes), shade and glazing room, water tank, spades, hoes, axes, furniture and fittings, other tools

## Market

The construction sector is one of the most vibrant in Uganda, registering the highest level of growth. Half bricks are in rising demand across both residential and commercial construction. The 7% margin is thin; viability depends on high volume and low transport costs. Location near active construction sites is critical.
