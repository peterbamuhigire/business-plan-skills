## Operations

- **Production Capacity:** 59,800 seasoned wood/timber per month; 717,600 products per year (100 electric poles + 2,200 fencing poles per day)

## Market

Seasoned wood and timber products (electric poles and fencing poles) serve the construction and infrastructure sectors. Electric poles for rural electrification programmes and fencing poles for agricultural and residential use provide two distinct market channels. Revenue includes electric poles ($742,900 adjusted) plus fencing pole revenue.

## Data Quality Notes

The gap between revenue and operating costs suggests strong gross margins, but capital equipment maintenance and timber sourcing costs may reduce net profitability.
