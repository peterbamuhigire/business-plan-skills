## Market

Vitrified clay pipe is one of the most durable sewer products available — long-lasting, environmentally friendly, and resistant to chemicals. Clay pipes are used in laying drainage lines for residential, commercial, and municipal infrastructure. Growing urbanisation drives demand for drainage infrastructure. Clay pipes compete with PVC in the drainage market; vitrified clay commands a premium for durability but requires kiln infrastructure.
