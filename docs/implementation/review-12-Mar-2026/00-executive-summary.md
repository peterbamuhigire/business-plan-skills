## Top 5 Critical Gaps

1. **Corporate governance / company structure depth** — `02-company-overview` is 60 lines with one reference. No guidance on shareholders' agreements, founder vesting, governance for growing companies, family business dynamics. Affects every Uganda SME plan.

2. **Business valuation** — No valuation framework anywhere in the suite. Equity investors ask "what is your company worth?" and the suite has no answer. DCF, comparables, pre-revenue valuation, cap table mechanics are all absent.

3. **Sustainability / ESG / ESMP** — DFI funding (UDB, IFC, EU grants) increasingly requires Environmental and Social Management Plans. The suite has no ESMP template, no ESG framework, and no sustainability reporting guidance. Identified as a known gap since March 2026 batch.

4. **Missing industry sectors** — Healthcare/clinics, real estate/property, transport/logistics (formal sector), ICT/tech startups, and renewable energy have NO industry guides. These are top-5 Uganda investment sectors.

5. **AI Integration section lacks book grounding** — `14-ai-integration` (124 lines) has zero book citations for AI business strategy. Every other flagship section is anchored to 5–14 cited works. This section is the least authoritative in the suite.

## Recommended Immediate Actions

1. Find a corporate governance / company structure book → enhance `02-company-overview`
2. Find a business valuation book → new `11c-valuation` skill or enhance `11-funding-request`
3. Find an ESG/sustainability framework → new reference files + ESMP template
4. Find an AI-for-business strategy book → ground `14-ai-integration`
5. Source healthcare, real estate, transport, ICT, and energy industry references → five new guides
