## Summary Verdict

The suite is **strong on strategy and marketing** but **weak on credit and legal fundamentals**. A plan generated today could win a pitch competition but would struggle to pass a bank credit committee or qualify for development finance. The three most dangerous gaps are:

1. **Section 11 (Funding Request)** has no East African lending knowledge — it treats all funding as venture equity, which is wrong for 90% of EA businesses that seek bank loans or development finance.
2. **Section 02 (Company Overview)** has no Uganda/EA legal structure guidance, no URSB registration process, no compliance checklist — the section a lender reviews to assess legal standing.
3. **No credit assessment framework** anywhere in the suite — CAMPARI, 5 Cs of credit, DSCR, and debt service capacity are the core lender decision criteria and are completely absent.
