## What the March 9 Analysis Correctly Predicted

Every Phase 1 critical gap was correctly identified. The prioritisation was accurate — DSCR, CAMPARI, collateral, and the Uganda tax framework were indeed the items that would cause immediate funding rejection, and they have all been addressed.

The Phase 2 and Phase 3 gaps were also correctly ranked by importance. The only unresolved gap from Phase 2 is the Uganda insurance framework (Action 2.7), which is the least critical of the Phase 2 items.
