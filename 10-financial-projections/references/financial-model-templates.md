*Ready-to-use financial statement templates calibrated to Uganda's tax regime and reporting requirements. Use these templates as the structural skeleton for all financial projections in Section 10. Fill in the business-specific figures; do not alter the row structure without good reason.*

**Tax treatment note (Uganda):**
- **Sole trader:** Apply PAYE individual tax bands to taxable profit (after allowable deductions). See PAYE bands in `references/uganda-tax-framework.md`.
- **Company:** Corporate income tax 30% on taxable profit. See `references/uganda-tax-framework.md` Section 2.
- **New citizen-owned company (≤ UGX 500M capital):** 0% for first 3 years (Income Tax Act amendment 2025). State this explicitly in the assumptions.
- **Presumptive tax (turnover < UGX 150M):** Flat annual rate instead of 30% corporate. See Section 6 of `references/uganda-tax-framework.md`.

**Cash flow timing rules for Uganda:**
- VAT: Collected monthly; net payment to URA due 15th of the following month
- PAYE: Deducted from employee salaries; remitted to URA by 15th of following month
- NSSF: Deducted + added; remitted to NSSF by 15th of following month
- WHT: Deducted at source by payer on applicable payments (rent, professional fees, etc.)
- Provisional income tax: Two instalments — 6th month and 12th month of financial year
- Final income tax: Due within 6 months of financial year end
